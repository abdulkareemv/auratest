About AURA 2K26

AURA 2K26 is the flagship state-level Technical and Management Fest of Government Women’s Polytechnic College, Kottakkal, bringing together some of the brightest minds from across the region to compete, innovate, and demonstrate technical brilliance and managerial excellence.

Building on the resounding success of AURA 2K25, which witnessed enthusiastic participation and widespread appreciation, AURA 2K26 returns with elevated standards, bigger challenges, and a more immersive experience—promising a dynamic platform for learning, creativity, collaboration, and celebration.
